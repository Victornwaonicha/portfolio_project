About JusticeGuard

JusticeGuard is a web application that holds a special place in my heart. It was born out of my personal experience as a victim of police brutality. I believe that technology can be a powerful tool for expression and social change, and JusticeGuard is my way of contributing to the conversation surrounding police misconduct and accountability.

With JusticeGuard, my goal is to empower individuals to report incidents of police misconduct, share their stories, and promote transparency within law enforcement agencies. By providing a platform for community engagement, we can work together to bring about positive change and advocate for justice.
